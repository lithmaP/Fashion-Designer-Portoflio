Liviana Spade

Fashion Designer and personal stylist, Liviana Spade, provides wardrobe styling for clients including CEOs of Global 200 companies, start-up executives, artists, and TV celebrities. She brings 6 years of experience designing for major American fashion brands. She has styled global runway fashion shows, designed her own menswear and womens collections, and was a Senior Designer at Armani. Liviana supports the concept of 'sustainable fashion' and works towards it by supporting businesses in the fashion industry that perform ethical and environmentally friendly practices. She styles for a new generation of men and women who want to make a positive impact in their buying decisions and behaviors. It's truly an investment in our future .

"My mission is to empower men & women to look and feel their best, and to make positive and mindful buying decisions."
-Stephanie Stimmler
